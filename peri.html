The Fourth Trimester

Healing &
Renewal.

The perinatal period is a time of profound transition. We provide the clinical and emotional tools to help you navigate your recovery with confidence and care.

Postpartum Recovery

Recovery is a journey, not a destination. We focus on gentle physical restoration and mental well-being to help your body heal after the incredible feat of childbirth.

  • ✦ Gentle mobility and restorative movement guides.
  • ✦ Monitoring physical healing and managing fatigue.
  • ✦ Emotional check-ins to support mental health transitions.

Breastfeeding Support

Whether you are starting your journey or looking for troubleshooting, our evidence-based support ensures a comfortable and healthy experience for both you and your baby.

  • ✦ Latch techniques and positioning for comfort.
  • ✦ Nutritional guidance for healthy lactation.
  • ✦ Supporting your choice and finding a rhythm that works.

Hormonal Rebalancing

Significant hormonal shifts are natural postpartum. We help you stabilize your system through sleep hygiene, nutrition, and stress management.

  • ✦ Balancing cortisol levels to improve sleep quality.
  • ✦ Micronutrient support for mood stabilization.
  • ✦ Understanding the "baby blues" vs. postpartum needs.
